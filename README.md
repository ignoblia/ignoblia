## 🕯️ who i am

I am a **Black trans woman currently living in Iceland** 🇮🇸.

Moving across borders as a trans person is **incredibly daunting**. When I was navigating my own relocation, I realized how hard it is to find actual, actionable moving data. Most sites track legal rights, but they don't tell you how to survive the execution of the move.

**I use this GitHub space to turn abstract legal rights into structured, realistic survival data for the trans community.**

## 🌐 my projects

### 🗺️ [TransIT](./transit)
> *structured global relocation + visa guide*

Tracks hard financial data, minimum visa income thresholds (Iceland's **1M ISK rule**), student pathways, prescription portability, and local QOL metrics.

**→ [relocation site](https://ignoblia.github.io/transit)**

<br>

### 🧪 [TransDIY](./transdiy)
> *harm reduction + independent transition support*

Homebrew medical tracking, global peer‑to‑peer resource sharing for those locked out of formal systems.

**→ [DIY resource hub](https://ignoblia.github.io/transdiy)**
