## 🕯️ who i am

I am a **Black trans woman currently living in Iceland** 🇮🇸.

Moving across borders as a trans person is **incredibly daunting**. When I was navigating my own relocation, I realized how hard it is to find *actual*, *actionable* moving data. Most sites track legal rights — they don't tell you how to survive the execution of the move.

**I use this GitHub space to turn abstract legal rights into structured, realistic survival data for the trans community.**

## 🌐 projects

### 🗺️ [TransIT](https://github.com/ignoblia/transit)
> *structured global relocation + visa guide*

Tracks hard financial data, minimum visa income thresholds (Iceland's **1M ISK rule**), student pathways, prescription portability, and local QOL metrics.

<p align="right">
  <a href="https://ignoblia.github.io/transit"><b>→ relocation site</b></a>
</p>

<br>

### 🧪 [TransDIY](https://github.com/ignoblia/transdiy)
> *harm reduction + independent transition support*

Homebrew medical tracking, global peer‑to‑peer resource sharing for those locked out of formal systems.

## ⚙️ philosophy

This space survives on:

- **mutual aid** — shared knowledge lifts everyone
- **collective knowledge** — lived experience is data
- **turning legal text into executable steps** — theory without action helps no one
